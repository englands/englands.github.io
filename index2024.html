The University of South Florida will be hosting its 52nd Engineering EXPO February 9th and 10th, 2024. It will be from 9 AM to 4 PM on Friday, February 9th and 9 AM to 2 PM on Saturday, February 10th.



Starting as a high school recruiting event to showcase the College of Engineering in 1973, the Engineering EXPO has expanded to become a two-day event that takes place on Friday and Saturday in February. The event has also expanded to welcome students in grades K-12 from throughout the state on local field trips to learn about the various opportunities available in STEM.
